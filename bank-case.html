About X Bank

My research was based on three mobile banking applications. In fact, my prototype is something in between what is now on the market with all the features that should be in a modern mobile bank

I decided to use a minimum of color in my project so that the user would be focused on the operations of the application and his attention would not go to unnecessary design and illustrations

In the design and layout of the functions of the application have been significant changes because I originally decided to use a schematic style to display the data, but after analyzing the competition I came to the conclusion that in this segment it is better to abandon unnecessary illustrations and graphs and charts if possible and eventually I came to this
