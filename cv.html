About me

My career encompasses the entire website development cycle, from design and competitor analysis to teamwork and project implementation. I am proficient in design tools such as Figma, Photoshop, Illustrator, and specialized platforms for website coding like Webflow and Readymag. My experience with After Effects enables me to incorporate dynamic elements into designs. I am skilled at creating mind maps and empathy maps, planning and designing wireframes, working with bitmap and vector images, and developing components and UI kits. Adaptive design is an integral part of my experience, allowing me to create websites that display perfectly on any device. The constant use of AI tools for enhancing analysis and assisting in design development improves the quality and efficiency of my projects.
