UI/UX/BRANDING/DEVELOPMENT/PRINT PRODUCTION
In this project, I encompass the full cycle of website development, from design and competitor analysis to teamwork and project execution. I employ design tools such as Figma, Photoshop, and Illustrator, and for web development, I use Webflow. Leveraging my experience with After Effects, I integrate dynamic elements into the design. I excel at creating mind maps and empathy maps, planning and designing wireframes, working with both bitmap and vector images, and developing components and UI kits. Additionally, I have expertise in adaptive design, ensuring that websites display correctly across various devices. Utilizing artificial intelligence tools to improve analysis and aid in development substantially boosts the quality and productivity of the projects.
